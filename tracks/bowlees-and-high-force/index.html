Bowlees & High Force

· 1 min read · December 15, 2025 ·

A short walk from Bowlees past Low Force to High Force and back, the two Forces in question being waterfalls. Wet underfoot—there had clearly been a lot of rain recently and my shoes turned out to leak slightly—but not nearly as wet on my head as I’d feared.
